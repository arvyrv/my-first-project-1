About Fitness Apparel

Given the practical nature of gym wear over other forms of menswear, there are not too many rules to abide by. The most important factors are fit, colour and style that need to be addressed.

When training in the gym, whether it be weights, cardio, yoga or a heavy HIIT class, it’s important to ensure that your outfit fits perfectly. And by perfect we mean clothes that give your arms, legs, waist and mid-section a full range of motion to take on any exercise. That’s not to say you need to be rocking the loosest fitting t-shirt and shorts in your wardrobe. Many brands, especially the ones we’ve listed here, now come in a slimmer cut to provide a cleaner aesthetic without sacrificing performance. Elastane and polyester blends help a lot with this so keep an eye on these types of garments which often come in joggers, shirts and compression gear.
